Meet the Team!

Hi, my name is Laney, and I'm the creator of this website! I love plane spotting, and I think its very entertaining and interesting. I want to teach everyone how to plane spot because it's such a fun hobby to do. It also feels great knowing what your watching and what plane it is. You also feel glad when you get the aircraft correct, almost like getting a question correctly on a quiz. You can also show off to your friends or family too! I started being interested in plane spotting not until a few months ago. I encourage everyone to get into plane spotting because it's honestly the best. It also makes you excited to go to the airport if your going on a trip! And if your flight gets delayed, you get a front row seat of plane spotting!
