Plane Spotting

Believe it or not, not every plane says A350 on the side of it like the plane in the home page. Plane Spotting is using photography to track all different kinds of aircraft. Plane spotters also gain information using airline routes, Air Traffic Control communications, and airport knowledge. You can plane spot whenever and at any airport. Plane spotting includes all aircraft, even the tiny planes.

If you are unable to plane spot or just don't want to go outside, there are plenty YouTube channels where they go out and record all the planes landing and taking off. Since we are near LAX, two popular channels that I know of go out almost everyday and plane spot for us for free at LAX! One is called L.A FLIGHTS and another is AIRLINE VIDEOS. They are two great channels with a great community of plane spotters! They include Air Traffic Control information and airplane route information as well. You can also test your knowledge and see if you can identify planes correctly there as well!
